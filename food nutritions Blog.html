Building a healthy Plate

Why a Balanced Meal Matters

A healthy plate helps us eat a balanced and nutritious meal. Half of the plate should be filled with fruits and vegetables because they provide important vitamins and minerals. One-quarter of the plate should contain whole grains, such as brown rice or whole-wheat bread, to give us energy. The remaining quarter should include protein-rich foods like chicken, fish, eggs, beans, or tofu to help our bodies grow and repair muscles. Drinking water and eating dairy products, such as milk or yogurt, also help keep us healthy and strong.

Nutrition tips for students

Drink enough water

Water keeps our body hydrated. It helps regulate body temperature, carries nutrients, aids digestion, removes waste, and keeps our brain and muscles working properly. Drinking enough water helps us stay healthy, active, and focused.

Eat colourful Food

Different colours in fruits and vegetables give different nutrients. A colourful Plate often means a healthier plate.
