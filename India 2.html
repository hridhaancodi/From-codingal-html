About India

Unity in diversity is the principle that harmony and cohesion can exist among people or groups despite differences in culture, religion, language, or ideology.

India is a vast South Asian country, the world’s most populous democracy, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and rapid socio-economic growth.

About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a 17th-century ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

About Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is a monumental arch in Mumbai, built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary in 1911, symbolizing India's colonial past and independence.

Taj Palace Hotel

The Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai, a heritage luxury hotel located in the Colaba area, is renowned for its stunning views of the Arabian Sea and the Gateway of India. Opened in 1903, it is one of the most iconic hotels in India
