Planet earth

Earth is the only known planet that sustains life, thanks to its unique combination of liquid water, atmosphere, and moderate temperatures.
It orbits the Sun at an average distance of about 149.6 million kilometers (93 million miles) and takes approximately 365.25 days to complete one revolution. Its rotation on its axis defines a 24-hour day, creating cycles of day and night.
