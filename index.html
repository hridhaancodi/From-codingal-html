About my school

Gandhi Memorial Intercontinental School (GMIS) Jakarta is a private international school established in 1950, located in the heart of Central Jakarta. It offers a diverse curriculum that includes the International Baccalaureate (IB) Primary Years Programme, Middle Years Programme, and Diploma Programme, as well as the Cambridge IGCSE. The school has over 2,000 students from more than 50 countries and emphasizes academic excellence, character development, and global citizenship. GMIS Jakarta is known for its multicultural environment and commitment to values-based education, preparing students for success in both academic and personal aspects.

How many activities and subjects for kids

GMIS Jakarta offers a well-rounded education with a broad curriculum that includes English, Mathematics, Science, Languages, Social Studies, Arts, Music, Design, Technology, and Physical Education through the IB and Cambridge programmes. Alongside academics, students can choose from more than 20 extracurricular activities, including sports, robotics, coding, debate, music, drama, dance, and community service. This balanced approach helps students develop academically, creatively, physically, and socially.
